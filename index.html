How It Works

1. Discover popular dishes near you

We partner with top food bloggers in NYC to bring you the most mouth-watering dishes that you can order online. Browse the curated selections on our mobile-first app. You can browse by influencer and neighborhood!

2. Click on the picture to order

People prefer to order food by looking at pictures. A picture = A thousand cravings. Browse our feed or use the search to find the dish you're craving for!

3. Complete the payment

Choose the most convenient method of payment for you from credit card to Google Pay, or Apple Pay. Once done, the food will be delivered to you as soon as it is ready.
